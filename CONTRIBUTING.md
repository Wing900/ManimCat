# Contributing to ManimCat

Thanks for considering a contribution.

## Contribution license

ManimCat no longer requires a Contributor License Agreement (CLA).

By submitting a contribution to this repository, you represent that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution;
- you are not knowingly including malicious code, obfuscated payloads, credential harvesters, or unrelated high-risk changes;
- your contribution may be distributed under the license scope defined in `LICENSE_POLICY.md`.

## Contribution expectations

- Keep pull requests focused on their stated purpose.
- Clearly identify any third-party code, assets, or data.
- Ensure any third-party material is compatible with the applicable license scope of the files it affects.
